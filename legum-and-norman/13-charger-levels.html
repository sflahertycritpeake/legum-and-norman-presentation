CHARGER LEVELS EXPLAINED

Maryland Right-to-Charge Law

Maryland law (effective Oct 2021) prohibits HOAs & condos from unreasonably restricting EV charger installation. Being proactive puts you ahead of resident demand.

Level 1

120V AC · Standard Outlet

1.2 kW output

3–5 miles of range added per hour

Slowest
8–20 hrs to full charge

Best for overnight, low-mileage use

Recommended
Level 2

240V AC · Dedicated Charger

7–19 kW output

10–60 miles of range added per hour

Moderate
4–8 hrs to full charge

Ideal for overnight or daytime parking

Level 3

480V DC · Fast Charge

50–350 kW output

100–200+ miles of range per hour

Fastest
20–60 min to 80% charge

For high-throughput, transient drivers
